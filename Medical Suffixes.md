# **Medical Suffixes**  

**1. ac, ic, ar, ary, ous = referring to/ pertaining to**  
  * Celiac = referring to bowels  
  * Gastric = related to stomach  
  * Venous = referring to veins  

**2. algia = pain**  
  * Neuralgia = pain along a nerve's path  

**3. apheresis = remove blood from the body, filter some components out, and then return the blood back to the body**  
  * Aquapheresis = removes excess water from the body  

**4. asthenia = weakness of something**  
  * Myasthenia gravis = weakness of the muscles.  

**5. centesis = to puncture surgically**  
  * Amniocentasts = amniotic sac is punctured surgically & fluid is removed for testing  

**6. cide = killer**  
  * Fungicide = to kill fungus causing an infection  

**7. crine = secrete**  
  * Eccrine glands = secrete out sweat  

**8. Cyte = cell**  
  * Erythricyte = red(erthryo) cell (blood cell)  

**9. drome = running or occurring**  
  * syndrome = signs or syptoms that are running or occurring together  

**10. ectasis = to expand or dilate**  
  * Angiectasis = dilation of a blood vessel  

**11. ectomy = to cut out**  
  * Tonsillectomy = surgically cut out the tonsils  

**12. emia = pertaining to blood**  
  * Anemia = not enough blood cells  

**13. ferrent = to carry**  
  * Afferent = nerves carry signals to the CNS  
  * Efferent = nerves carr signals away from the CNS  

**14. fuge = drive away**  
  * Centrifuge = spins blood around to drive away red blood cells to one side  

**15. gen = producing or causing something**  
  * Carcinogen = causing cancer  

**16. gram = to record, picture, or draw something**  
  * Mammogram = an x-ray image of breast tissue to detect cancer.  

**17. graphy = writing or recording**  
  * Radiography = records images of the body  

**18. ia = refers to a condition**  
  * Pneumonia  = a condition affecting the lungs  

**19. iatrics = a speciality**  
  * Pediatrics = specializing in children  

**20. ism , osis, ty = state or condition of something**  
  * Hypothyrodism = state of having an underactive thyroid  
  * Scoliosis = state of having a crooked spine  
  * Immunity = a state of being immune to something  

**21. itis = Inflammation**  
  * Dermatitis = inflammation of the skin(dermis)  

**22. logy, logist = the study of something or one who studies**  
  * Nephrologist = studies the kidney  
  * Biology  the study of life  

**23. lysis = something breaking down or apart**  
  * Hemolysis = breaking down of red blood cells.  

**24. mania = consumed or obsessed with something**  
  * Kleptomania = the obsession with stealing items  

**25. megaly = enlargement**  
  * Splenomegaly = enlarged spleen  

**26. oma = tumor or growth**  
  * Sarcoma = type of tmor that arid=ses from connective tissue  

**27. opia = vision**  
  * Myopia = when vision is good at looking at objects up close, but not so looking at objects far away  

**28. pathy = disease or suffering**  
  * Neuropathy = disease of the nerves  

**29. paresis = weak or paralyzed**  
  * Gastroparesis = stomach seizes up and delays emptying its contents  

**30. penia = something is decreased or lacking**  
  * Leukopenia = decrease in white blood cells  

**31. pepsia = digestion**  
  * Dyspesia = indigestion  

**32. phagia = eating or consuming**  
  * Dysphagia = difficulty eating or swallowing  

**33. phobia = fear**  
  * Lilapsophobia = fear of tornadoes or severe storms  

**34. plasia = growth or development of something**  
  * Hyperplasia = cells in an organ begin to grow or develop abnormally 

**35. plasm = living part or the substance of something**  
  * Cytoplasm = the substance that makes up a cell  

**36. plasty = to reshape something**  
  * Rhinoplasty = reshaping of the nose  

**37. plegia = paralysis**  
  * Quadraplegia = paralysis affecting all four limbs  

**38. pnea = breathing**  
  * Sleep apnea = temporarily stop breathing during sleeping.  

**39. poesis = producing or making**  
  * Erythr0poesis = body's process of making red blood cells  

**40. ptosis = sagging or dropping down**  
  * Hysteroptosis = having a lot of babies can cause the uterus to drop down or sag  

**41. rrhage = heavy bleeding**  
  * Hemorrhage = bleeding  

**42. rrhagia = heavy or abnormal discharge of something**  
  * Menorrhagia = abnormally long, heavy period  

**43. rrhrea = sudden discharge of something**  
  * Diarrhea = sudden discharge of stool  

**44. sclerosis = hardening or suffering of something**  
  * Atherosclerosis = plaque builds up and causes hardening of the arteries.  

**45. scope = tool used to help you see**  
  * scopy = examination with that tool  
  * During a colonoscopy, the colon is examined using a colonoscope.  

**46. spasm = twitching or contracting of muscle tissue**  
  * Bronchospasm = muscles of the bronchi begin to contract involuntarily  

**47. stasis = ceasing balancing something**  
  * Homeostasis = bidy always tries to maintain a proper balance  

**48. stenosis = something is narrowing or closing in**  
  * Renal artery stenosis = this artery narrows which limits blood flow to kidney and increases blood pressure  

**49. stomy = openinf=g created in something**  
  * Colostomy = opening created in the colon  

**50. tocia = birth or the process of birth**  
  * Dystocia = difficult birhting process  

**51. tomy = to  cut something**  
  * Hysterectomy = to cut out the uterus  

**53. trophy = growth or nourishment**  
  * If ypu stop working out, your muscle may atrophy(stop growing).

**54. uria = urine**  
  * Hematuria = blood present in the urine
